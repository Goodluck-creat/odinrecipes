White rice and stew

DESCRIPTION

White Rice and Stew is one of the most popular Nigerian Food.

white Rice recipe is common because it can be eaten with any stew or sauce of your choice.

INGREDIENTS

  • the White Rice; 1tsp salt for the rice.
  • Meat
  • stocks
  • blended Tomatoes
  • Chilli pepper to taste etc

STEPS

  1. Boil the rice with salt until is cooked and dry
  2. Season meat properly to get the stock
  3. When it is cooked, you could fry or grill the meat if you like
  4. Pour the vegetable oil into a pot and place on heat
  5. Throw in the chopped onions when the oil is hot
  6. Pour the blended tomatoes into the pot and allow to cook for about 10mins
  7. Pour in the stock and cook further
  8. Add the seasoning cube, ginger, crushed garlic, curry and thyme
  9. Add the meat and salt to taste
  10. for another 20mins
  11. Add the meat and salt to taste
